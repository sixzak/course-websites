Week 8
Next week (starting 4/15) will be the Midterm written exam
You must know:
1) Answers to every question on the "Questions" web page and More (other important ideas). Think about information discussed in class and ideas from your homework and other classmates.
2) Themes for all 3 stories (general, complete idea) & explanations (specific information from story that explains/proves the theme)
3) All Betty answers
4) Memorize the one Haiku (we chose in class) and 3 opinion adjectives (feelings that the haiku poem creates). You will write the memorized haiku and the opinion adjectives on your exam.

Questions

The Ones Who Walk Away from Omelas
Describe the processions of the Festival of Summer.
What does the Omelas society not have?
Why was the child so thin?
Where do the people who walk away from Omelas go?

The Story of the Goblins Who Stole a Sexton
Why did Gabriel hit the boy?
What did the goblins play with the tombstones?
What did the goblin king give Gabriel to drink?
What was found in the churchyard after Gabriel was gone?

EPICAC
It took EPICAC a split second to solve what kind of problems?
After Pat said that mathematicians were all hearts and flowers, what did she say?
What lies does the narrator tell EPICAC?
What does EPICAC give the narrator and Pat?
