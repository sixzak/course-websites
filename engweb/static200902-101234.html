Week 15
For the new few weeks, work on your presentation, final written exam and video:
Project - Next week (starting June 3)
You will be showing your presentations (ppt) in class. Practice them at home and with your group and discuss about which parts you will be speaking, and how your group will operate the ppt. Of course no microphones will be used in class. Make sure you have an effective presentation that includes exciting acting (emotions, gestures, looking at audience while speaking)
Please upload your presentations (ppt) to TronClass at least 15 minutes before class starts and do not come late to class because we want to start on time.
Self learning - EngNet (not available) You should already have your 4 points done, but it should be possible to still get points this week
Blogs - will be closed after week 14 (The Last Leaf-b), so make sure to write in them for the last time this week. (You will not write in them starting week 15 - focus on presentation, exam, video)
Final written exam - start reviewing your material to make sure you have the answers to story and Betty questions. We will discuss the exam next week when the presentations are over.
Video - keep practicing all of your own lines at home, but do not start making the video until after you are ready with your presentations. Your video will have to be finished and uploaded to YouTube on or before June 17, so you will still have a little time to finish it after the final written exam (if your group wants to wait).

Week 16
Next week (starting 6/10) is the final written exam - make sure you know:
a) Answers to all story questions on the Questions page (and other information from each of the stories - think about what you learned in class).
b) Answers to all Betty homework questions
c) Choose two of the stories on the Questions page and write a theme (phrase, general) and explanation (3-5 sentences, complete, explains the theme)
and
for the story you did't choose to write themes/explanations for, you will write a haiku about one event that happens anywhere in the story (3 lines, don't use character names) - then write a short (3-5 sentences) explanation for the haiku poem - what happens during the event and what is the feeling.
Self learning - EngNet (not available) You should already have your 4 points done.
Blogs - no more blog writing
Video - Your video will have to be finished, uploaded to YouTube with a link on TronClass on the week after the final exam. You will also have to watch some other groups' videos and fill out a short questionnaire.

Please finish this questionnaire before Monday, June 24 at 12:00 (noon) - you can fill it out as soon as you watch all the videos in your section.
