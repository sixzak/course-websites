Questions

Know the answers to the following questions (and more):

Some of Us Had Been Threatening Our Friend Colby
What did Howard say about Ives Fourth Symphony?
What did Tomas sketch on the backs of envelopes?
What business did Harry run?
What did Colby stand on when he died?

The Second Bakery Attack
After the couple woke up, what did they find?
What did the two men have to do to get the bread?
How did the wife describe the presence of the curse?
Before the wife said, Were stealing bread what did she do?

The Last Leaf
What did Sue say that Johnsy wanted to do?
The doctor will give Johnsy a one-in-five chance if she does what?
After Johnsy sees that the last leaf did not fall, what did she do?
What did the janitor find?
