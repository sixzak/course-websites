Weekly tasks

Weekly preparation from midterm to final

Week (4/21)
This week we learned about "what babies know" - don't throw away your notes (remember this knowledge - be able to summarize the results).
Homework- For next class we will learn about FOXP2 - spend 5 minutes searching the internet (googling) about it. Write it down. Be able to tell me something. 5 minutes is enough (do not spend more time than that).

Week (4/28)
For next week, download Vygotsky ↓ ↓viewview - read it, answer the homework questions, (small groups - ok) and be ready to discuss it in class.
Homework will be handed in at the beginning of class (so write the homework answers on a separate piece of pager).

Week (5/05)
For next week, download Bloom's Taxonomy ↓ ↓viewview - read it, answer the homework questions, (small groups - ok) and be ready to discuss it in class.
Homework will be handed in at the beginning of class (so write the homework answers on a separate piece of pager).

Week (5/12)
For next week, download Baby Talk ↓ ↓viewview - read it, and be able to tell me the main ideas in class. No written homework - just be able to tell me the main ideas.

Week (5/17)
For next week, download Multiple Intelligences ↓ ↓viewview - read it, answer the homework questions, (small groups - ok) and be ready to discuss it in class.
Homework will be handed in at the beginning of class (so write the homework answers on a separate piece of pager).

Week (5/24)
1) For next class - just bring Bilingualism ↓ ↓viewview (if you have time).
If you don't bring it in it is OK, but you will still need to know the information.
2) Make sure you have talked to your group members about the Final Project (read the web page).
3) Don't wait to learn the material for the final exam (learn all homework and "other questions" answers.

Week (6/03)
For next week have your videos ready and bring them to class. Look at the Final Project web page to understand the project more (Make them better, more useful and more interesting than the midterm videos - but have fun, too).
Take all questions from homework and other questions and write "key word" answers (talk to your group - talk to other groups). Bring to class to share and discuss.

Week (6/10)
Next week is the final written exam. Many of you have not prepared the question and answer "key word" answers review sheets, yet. Work with your group members to find the answers to all questions, then talk to other groups.
Study the questions and answers every day (not just the day before the exam). Make sure you can say the answers without reading them. Try to pay attention to the topic (for example is the question from FOXP2, or Vygotsky, or from Bloom's Taxonomy?).

*Some of you need to do very, very well on this exam. Study hard and good luck.*
