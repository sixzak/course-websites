Drama Preparation and Rehearsal

 

Researching, rehearsing and documenting a dramatic performance will be a challenging task that will require all the time we can give it.

All actors will be required to attend 2 weekly rehearsal sessions: Mondays 10:00 to 12:00 and Thursdays 12:30 to 3:00. These are the minimum rehearsal times and will be expanded as needed.

In addition, small group teams should continue on documentation (written record), music/dance and any artwork necessary.

Put in as much time as you can and you will enjoy the final result.
