Pumpkin swirled cheese cake
Pumpkin cheese cake is a delicious snack to make at home. First, preheat oven to 190℃. Second, mix crushed cookies, 3 tablespoons of melted butter and 3 tablespoons of flour in a bowl. Third, press firmly on bottom and side of ungreased 9 inch pie plate, and bake about 12 minutes. Then, allow to cool. Fourth, combine white sugar, brown sugar, flour, and cream cheese in a larger bowl, and beat on low speed until smooth. Fifth, reserve 1/2 cup of this mixture to swirl in later. Sixth, add vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger to the mixture in the bowl. Then, blend in eggs and pumpkin puree. Seventh, scrape bowl, and beat until smooth, and pour into crust. Eighth, stir 1 tablespoon milk into the reserved cream cheese mixture, and drop by full spoon over the pumpkin mixture. Then, use a knife to decoratively swirl the two mixtures together. Tenth, cover edge of crust with 2 to 3 inch strip of aluminum foil to prevent excessive browning. Then, bake in preheated 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Eleventh, remove foil the last 15 minutes of baking. The final step, cool down 30 minutes, then refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving. At last, enjoy the yummy food. In Beijing, there is an interesting food transit way. As we know, some restaurant offer food delivering services, but what if you want to order a meal from a restaurant which doesn’t supply this service? Never mind if you live in Beijing. You may order any restaurant and use an app to tell the rider to get it for you, then get the meal when he arrived your front door with an extra little fee. Because of Beijing’s serious traffic problems, most people would rather stay home then go out for food, so food delivering jobs get popular in a short time. This way of transport not only saved tons of time and also decreases global warming, because the deliver riders only ride electric motors, since Beijing haven’t release any gasoline motor licenses for decades, they truly helped decreasing the temperature of Earth.
