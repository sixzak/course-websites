(1.) recipe

“Fake” Pork Roast
First, chop potatoes in half and steam it that soft chopsticks can easily get into it. When we steam the potatoes, dice the onions and mushrooms into pieces and sauté mushrooms and onions with butter. They'll ready to go when you smell the fragrance. After the potatoes finish, put them into cold water. This will make skinning the potatoes a lot easier. Next, put the potatoes in a large bowl, mash them and add the mushrooms and onions we have sautéd and mix them well. When it almost done, season with salt and shape them it to oval like a huge egg.
Second, put several bacon strips into rectangle and put another several bacon strips overlap them, make it like a cross with 3 or 4 bacon strips width. Next, wrap the mashed potatoes with the cross bacon strips, sprinkle some rosemary, use rope truss it, be sure the shape won't break and bake it with 400oF for 40 to 45 minutes. This is our pork roast. Meanwhile, pour some red wine, soy sauce and mirin into a pan. You can play with the ratio of each ingredient. Boil them for a couple minutes, add in some butter and wait it melt in.
Finally, put the pork roast into dish and remove the rope, pour the sauce that we make in the pan over pork roast evenly. Now, let's enjoy our “fake” pork roast.

(1.) Opinion Paragraph

How do movies, television or video games influence the stories qualities?

There's no higher of stories, only the different of the skills to present the stories. Whether movie, television or video game is a carrier of the story ,in other words these are all one type of platform of stories and how to build on it comes to be the most important problem to solve. In my opinion, these skills can be classified as three different points, Character growing, Rendering of emotion and Tempo control.
As an author, you are not only build your characters, but also let them grow up in the story line. In the story, after every events, every characters must have his or her own emotion, own reaction, own opinion and his own thought to face the event. Those should transform to experience and makes this character stand to the next stage. It will help audiences get into the role, their emotion will synchronize with the character, so them was moved.
To support the Character growing, Rendering of emotion is the perfect trigger to trigger the ripple of audiences deep subconscious. It can be using construction of image, back ground music and time delay of screams to let audiences into a trace. The audiences should feel like all of light, sound and people around are going far and far away, then all focus are on the story and the characters.
Last but not least an author must make story be good at the overall situation. The skill in that level is the Tempo control, an easy way to explain it is the application of aftertaste. Such as, in story line, put A part before B part and make the time between these two parts be appropriate. Use A part's aftertaste to strong the emotion feeling of B part. Make information gets in to audiences in the right timing. Like an horror story, use the aftertaste of something can't identified show up, then make audiences be scared by just wind, cloud or even themselves.
No matter what kind of platform you have used to write a story, I believe that if we can make good use of these three techniques, even the story line was not wonderful and the platform was not the most suitable one, the way of perform can make the story perfect.
