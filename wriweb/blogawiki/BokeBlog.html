Golden Fried Rice
Golden Fried Rice usually use overnight cooked rice. First heat the rice and put some yolk into the rice.
Then, mix the yolk and rice til the rice turn yellow. Next, heat the oil, onions , green onions, and corn kernels in a flying pan.
After that, put the rice into the pan and fry it.
When the rice turn gold, add a little salt and soy sause if you don’t want your meal become too salty to eat.
The last step, put the rice into a plate to finish it.
