So they don’t study.They watch TV series ,read novels ,have parties with friends and so on. They tell themselves, I hate it so I don’t want to learn it and it’s impossible for me to learn well. Recipe
Fried rice with egg is a simple meal which is suitable for beginner. First,wash the vegetables and cut them into one or two-centimeter pieces. Next fry the egg and use truner to cut it into small pieces. At that moment, you need to pay attention. You should put the cold rice into the pot when the eggs don't completely cooked. Then stir the rice and egg and put vegetables into the pot. After that, continue to stir them.When you think they are all cooked, you can put them into a bowl. Now you can enjoy the fruit of your labor.

In my opinion, the most livable city is Zhuhai, a seaside city next to Macao in Guangdong Province. First, the economic strength of Zhuhai ranks in the top few in Guangdong Province,which means it has lots of living infrastructure, such as supermarkets, shopping centers and so on. The population is not large. So traffic jam is not very often. What’s more,the air is fresh,the sky is blue and the street is clean,which makes you comfortable and relaxed. Because of these, it’s a relaxing city which is very suitable for living especially for the old.

Week Seven
Opinion
In my opinion, a considerate and friendly person is a good neighbor.
First, he is understanding and considerate. In daily life, it’s inevitable to disturb others sometimes. We should be tolerant of others’ disturbance if it isn’t what they want. For example, when someone is renovating his home, he has to make a noise though he is sorry about it. He has tried his best to reduce the noise. At that time, understanding each others is important.
Second, he is kind and friendly. He is willing to help their neighbors when they have some troubles. There is an old saying: A near neighbor is better than a distant cousin. A good neighbor give us a hand like our family. He makes us warm and think we’re supported.
All in all, a good neighbor is a person passing warmth. Considerate and friendly, you will be others’ good neighbor and have new families.
