Recipe
''Fried tomato with egg''
Fried tomato with egg is a famous and simple Chinese food. Today I want to teach you how to make this delicious food. First you should to prepare for two tomatoes, four eggs, two scallion, a ketchup, sugar and salt. Then start to slice the tomatoes, beat the eggs, and chop scallion into several sections. Next, scramble the beaten eggs, as soon as the egg mixture start to thick, add scallion, tomato slices, catsup and seasoning. And finally, you can prepare for enjoy your simple delicious fried tomato with egg.
