Billiards competition
Last Friday I went to billiards class at school. And during the class, my teacher came here to the pool table of my team a NO.9 ball and ask me if I want to join the school billiard team for the coming competition. I finally decided to participate after thinking twice. We play Nine-ball in the competition. The rule of this game is we need to use the cue ball to push the ball into the bag. And we can only hit the ball following the number from one the nine. The person who push the NO.9 ball into the bag wins the game. Teacher also told me, professional player will hit all the ball into the bag because they are professional. But the participants are all students. Their skills are not as well as professional players. So we often use the cue ball to hit the smallest number’s ball, and let the smallest ball to push NO.9 ball into the bag. Although teacher thought I played billiard very well. But I think I need more and more practice to make my skill better. I hope I can give a great performance on the competition.

Week 3/15
Recipe of making corn soup instant noodles
In the dormitory, we can’t prepare too many ingredients. The easiest way to feed ourselves is making instant noodles. This corn soup instant noodles was invented by myself last semester. First, we have to prepare a can of corn soup, an egg and as many instant noodles as you want for a meal. Then, open the soup can and pour into the pot, add one more can of water before start to cook the soup. I will also put the egg into the soup to make it healthier. You can also add some salt because rather than soup, it’s more like a sauce. After finish the corn soup, you can start to boil the instant noodles. I often use the seasoning inside the noodles package to make the noodles taste better. If your corn soup is salty enough, you can skip this step. Then, mix the corn soup and instant noodles that just finish cooking. Try it and add more seasoning depending on the taste you like. Finally, you can enjoy the homemade corn soup instant noodles in the dormitory.

Opinion paragraph
Nowadays, mass media influence people a lot. But how do movies, television or video games influence people's behavior? In a lot of social news, we can see some teenagers have violent tendencies because they played too many video games about crimes and gangsters. People also get many strange information from movies and television. And these strange information indirectly cause their strange behavior.
