Caramel pudding
Serving size :4
Ingredients:
Caramel
150 g caster sugar
50 ml water
Custard
250 ml whole milk
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract 1
63 g caster sugar
125 g egg

Method
Caramel
First,put sugar and water in a saucepan, heating up until the syrup becomes golden brown. Stir occasionally. When you pour the syrup into ice water it should form a ball, indicating the correct consistency. Next ,Pour the caramelized syrup evenly into mouds and then let it cool.
Custard
First preheat the oven 150c,then Add the milk and vanilla into a saucepan and bring it to simmer(Remember not overheat the milk, otherwise when you combine your ingredients, you’ll end up with scramble eggs.)
Second ,In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, and vanilla extract. Pour the warm milk into egg mixture slowly and stir constantly. Third,strain the custard immediately, pour custard evenly into mouds.Final,bake the cream caramel in a water bath to keep the oven moisture high and the heat gentle. Bake for 25-35 minutes. When the surface of cream caramel solidifies, it’s done.
