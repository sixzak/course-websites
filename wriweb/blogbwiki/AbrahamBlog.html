Recipe
First of all, spray muffin pan with baking spray or nonstick cooking spray. Heat the oven to 375°. Next, in a mixing bowl, combine the flour, 1 cup granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt; blend well. In addition, in a small bowl, whisk together the vanilla, eggs, and cream or half-and-half. Add to the dry ingredients along with the melted butter and stir just until combined. Fold in the strawberries. Fill muffin cups about 2/3 full. Moreover, combine the topping ingredients, using a fork, pastry blender, or fingers, working until the mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle the top of each muffin with a generous amount of crumb topping. Finally, Bake the muffins for about 20 to 25 minutes, until set and a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean.
