Three-cup chicken is one of the famous Chinese dishes that my mother usually make. First, heat up the sesame oil in a wok, and then add garlic, ginger and pepper, stir-fry until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Second, add the chicken pieces and cook about 5 minutes until it turns white. Next, stir together the soy sauce, sugar and rice wine in a bowl, pour over chicken. Continue heating until it boiled, then turn down the heat to low. When the sauce has thickened, turn the heat back up to high and add basil and scallions, and cook a little while. Finally, Three-cup chicken is done.

1)Cause and Effect
A traffic accident happened because there was a moose that want to cross the road, and the woman didn’t notice. When she was going to bump into the moose, she turned the steering wheel to avoid it. Then, she rushed into the forest, so the front of the vehicle was almost crushed. After a while police arrived, he was confusing that how did the accident happen? At the same time, the woman who holds a baby raised her hand to call the police and tried to let the police know the situation of this accident. The accident was too scary, so the baby was crying loudly. Because the baby was too noisy, the police didn’t have the patience to listen to all things through that the women said. The car was broken, so the engine had a lot of smoke. It seemed very dangerous, so the woman really wanted to keep away from this scene to protect her baby. However, pitiful mother and baby couldn’t go home because they didn’t have another way to go home except their car. The innocent moose looked at this scene and didn’t know what it had done.
