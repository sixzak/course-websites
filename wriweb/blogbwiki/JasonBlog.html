The Iron Lady
Do you know who was the longest-serving British prime minister of the 20th century, and the first woman to have held the office ? Margaret Hilda Thatcher was a courageous and confident person who was the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1979 to 1990 and the Leader of the Conservative Party from 1975 to 1990. Her political philosophy and economic policies emphasised deregulation (particularly of the financial sector), flexible labour markets, the privatisation of state-owned companies, and reducing the power and influence of trade unions. After retiring from the Commons in 1992, she was given a life peerage as Baroness Thatcher which entitled her to sit in the House of Lords. In conclusion, she contributed her life and was the first stateswoman who was the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom and led the British away from the bad economy, that was why she called The Iron Lady.

Making Boiled Egg

Boiled egg is an easy dish to prepare. To make boiled egg, put a cookpot on the gas range. Then pour 500ml of water into cookpot, and wait for water is bowing. Next, put egg into boiling water. Finally, cover the cookpot and cook over low heat for about 10 ~ 20 minutes. If you follow this simple recipe, you will have a yummy meal!

How to use a Multimeter

A multimeter is a tool to test the electricity. If you do not know the plugs are working or broken, just use that to test plugs you can get answer. First, open the multimeter and check the battery. Then, turning the switch to the AC 220 V sign (V ̃) or above 220 V . Next, putting the red stick into one of the plug holes, and putting black one into another plug holes. Finally, if you see the value on the screen of multimeter, the power still works, or the line of plug is broken or switch still at off.
