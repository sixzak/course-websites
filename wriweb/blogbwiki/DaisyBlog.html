4. Here is an explanation of how to make a simple birthday cake with only a few required ingredients. First, at the grocery store, purchase a boxed cake mix and a container of frosting .You will also need eggs, oil, non-stick cooking spray, birthday candles matches or a lighter, oven mitt, and a cooling rack. Next wash your hands and work area. Read the directions on the cake box to be sure you have all the ingredients needed. Place the equipment and food items in your work area according to the order you will be using them. Be sure to preheat your oven to the appropriate temperature before you begin. Next, put the dry cake mix into the large mixing bowl. Add the prescribed amount of water, oil, and eggs to the mix. With the electric mixer or mixing spoon, mix the batter. Pour the cake batter into the cake pan, using the rubber spatula to scrape all of the batter off of the sides of the bowl. Finally, stick the appropriate number of candles into the cake’s top, light them, and present your delicious cake to the special, birthday guy or gal.
