Warm potato & Green bean salad
Warm potato & green bean salad is my favorite food, because it is easy for myself to make. First cook unpeeled potatoes covered in water until tender about 15 minutes, adding green beans to pan for last 5 minutes to barely cook. Second, drain and place in salad bowl. Then, add onion and pepper to bowl. Next, mix remaining dressing ingredients and pour over potato mix tossing gently to coat. The last step, let it sit at room temperature about 1 hour before serving. Now you can share with your friends.

4)
Kilt
Renowned item of Scottish culture is the kilt. This garment, a kind of skirt, is worn by Scottish men on formal occasions, as well as to show national pride at sporting events. It is usually worn with a sporran, a fur or leather pouch that serves in place of pockets, along with thick stockings and a ceremonial knife. The kilt has a tartan, or pattern of stripes, that can identify which clan or other association the wearer belongs to.
