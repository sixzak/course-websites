Recipe
Fried rice is one of my favorite delicacies. This cuisine has eight steps. First,
mix rice with salt, chicken powder and chop spring onion. Second, use medium heat to heat up the wok until dry. Third, remain the medium heat, then use ladle pour oil into wok and make sure the oil has spread evenly into wok. After that, wait till the wok is hot enough, pour over the egg into wok. Then, use spatula quickly to stir egg. Next, when egg cook to medium done then add in the prepared rice mixture. Next, quickly stir fried the rice mixture with egg. After the rice and egg are mixed well, add in the Iceberg lettuce slice and continued stir fried it. Then, belong your own dish finish.
