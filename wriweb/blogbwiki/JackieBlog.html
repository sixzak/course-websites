Learning Responsibility

During my working time, I not only helped customers find clothes, by which I learned to choose and order new clothing, but also answered the phone and checked the mail. What’s more, I must keep the store neat and clean. When I used the cash register and handle credit cards, I had to be careful. According to myself, dressing neatly and professionally was an important part of the job. These things all taught me responsibility. Though now I don’t work with clothing anymore, I still use that important skill I learned in my first job.

2) Italian nuts chocolate pretzel is a homely snack in Italy families and is not hard to DIY. First, using a whisk, mix the eggs, brown and white sugar to a smooth paste and look a little bit white. Then sieve the cake flour and baking soda and add them to the egg liquid. Next we can stir all of these together with walnuts and chocolate beans. After flattening the paste, an important step is to put it in the cold storage for an hour. Take it out for 30 minutes’ bake with 150°C. After cutting into small pieces, the final step is putting them into the oven for another 10 minutes’ bake with 160°C. Then delicious Italian nuts chocolate pretzel is OK to eat.
