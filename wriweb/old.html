Week 4/12
Next week 4/19 will be your written midterm exam. This week you need to write and prepare.
2 writing tasks:
1) Consider the following topics and think about your opinions regarding them. You can choose one of these topics or choose any topic and opinion that you want to write about. Write your opinion paragraph with a topic sentence, opinions and examples (facts), and a concluding sentence
a) If you could change one important thing about your hometown, what would you change?
b) How do movies, television or video games influence people's behavior?
c) Some people prefer to eat at food stands or restaurants. Other people prefer to prepare and eat food at home. (choose one and write your opinion)
d) Neighbors are the people who live near us. In your opinion, what are the qualities of a good (or bad) neighbor?
e) What are some important qualities of a good (or bad) supervisor/boss?
f) Any topic (including ideas from Betty)
2) Think about the writings by Haruki MurakamiView . Think about how he uses things to talk about some aspect of himself or his life. Write 2 sentences about yourself using something (not a living thing) to describe something about you.

Week 4/19
Next week 4/26 will be your midterm project.

2-3 minute PowerPoint presentation in English about:
1) A writer (author) - short description of writing habits, or his/her opinion about writing or short history (something in his or her life that influenced his or her writing- not a list of books, stories or awards).
2) His or her writing - some short sentences from something the writer has written
3) Why you chose these sentences (how they make you feel or how they are related to your life)
4) Something you write - based on the sentences of the writer - try to write similar to the writer (can be related to style or content) and explain the relationship of your writing to that of the author

* Practice speaking at home - loudly and with eye contact and good presentation. If you speak more than 3.5 minutes, I will say "stop". Remember all words - spoken and written must be in English.
Try to come to class as early as possible, so you can put your file on the computer desktop (name your file with your student number - ex. B23456789.pptx). After all of the files are ready, we will begin.
