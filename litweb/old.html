Week 11/02
Next week November 10 is our midterm exam. Make sure you know every answer to all of the questions we have discussed in class - you MUST do this. If you want to review more, talk to your group members and review the homework questions that you wrote for each story.

In addition, you must also know themes for 3 different stories and be able to explain them. This means:
1) Write the story name
2) Write the theme. Remember a theme is the idea behind the story - a main point the author is trying to make about life. Use your own words, do not mention the characters or the plot details.
3) Write a detail from the story plot that shows/explains the theme. This could be a few sentences that talk about what characters do. This should have a relationship to the theme (it should demonstrate the theme).

Which 3 stories?
One from the story that you did your midterm project (memorized sentences).
The other two are your choice (choose 2 stories that you understand or like).

Don't worry too much about this, just think about it and write things that make sense. Writing something is better than not writing anything.
