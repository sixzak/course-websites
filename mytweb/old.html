Week (11/02)
Today we reviewed last week's myth about Orpheus and Eurydice p2 and watched the midterm acting videos.
Next week November 12 is your midterm exam. You need to have the information in All QuestionsView understood and memorized well.
Your classmates and class leader Ju should be able to tell you the correct answers, but it is up to you to learn them.
Spend time every day reviewing the questions and memorizing the answers (focus on "key words").
Most of the exam will be from that material.

You must know the answers to these questions - talk to your classmates, share the answers, understand and learn them:

Olympian Creation
According to Greek Mythology, What was there at the beginning of creation?
Who are Gaia and Uranus? What happened to Uranus and why?
Who are Rhea and Cronus? What happened to Cronus and why?

After Zeus got his brothers and sisters back, what did he do next?
What did Mother Earth (Gaia) do? What happened?
What happened to Enceladus and what does he do?
Creation of Man
What job did Zeus give Prometheus and why?
What did Epimetheus give the animals?
What did Pometheus give man and what did Zeus do?
Pandora (play)
Who made Pandora?
What did other gods give her?
What did Zeus give her?
Who did she meet on Earth and why?
What did Pandora do?
What was left in the box?

Persephone
Why has Hades come to the upper world?
Why does Hades take Persephone to the underworld?
What does Demeter say she will do?
Why should Persephone not do? And why?
How does Hades trick Persephone?
What is the deal?
What did this myth explain to the ancient Greeks?

Daedalus and Icarus p1
How could Daedalus be the greatest craftsman in Greece?
What do Icarus and the dog think about Icarus?
Describe Talos.
What would Daedalus and the boys do in the evenings?
What happened to Talos?
What secrets did Daedalus know and what did King Minos do?
After they escaped from the Minotaur what couldn't they do?

Daedalus and Icarus p2
How did Daedalus make the wings?
What did Daedalus tell Icarus?
Who did Daedalus curse and why?
After Daedalus flew away from Crete, what happened?
How did Minos try to find Daedalus?
How did Daedalus solve the shell problem?
While Minos was planning Daedalus' execution, what did he do? And what happened?

Orpheus and Eurydice p1
Why was Orpheus the greatest musician the world had ever known?
Where did Orpheus find Eurydice?
How long were Orpheus and Eurydice married?
Why didn't Orpheus want Eurydice to go into the forest?
What happened as Aristaeus chased Eurydice?
After Eurydice was dead, what did Orpheus say about his lyre?
What did Orpheus decide to do?

Orpheus and Eurydice p2
What does Charon want from Eurydice?
How does Hades describe himself?
What does Hades tell Orpheus about taking Eurydice?
Why doesnt Charon want Orpheus to sing?
What did the dog think would happen?
Why did the women circle around Orpheus?
After Orpheus was torn limb from limb, what happened?
