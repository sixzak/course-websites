News story video

Your group will be choosing a CNN 10 story from the ones we discussed in class.
Your group will be using the transcript (exact words) to make your own video. If the transcript uses (inaudible) or (ph), use the correct words from the actual CNN video.
Remember:
You may not use any images (still or video) from the actual CNN video (you can use other images that you can find in other places)
All group members must speak (try to divide the work evenly) and each member must show his or her face while speaking for at least one sentence.
*Think of ways to make your video interesting and effective*
